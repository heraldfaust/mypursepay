Who is MyPurse for?


MyPurse is designed for every Nigerian who wants to take control of their money. But there are a few folks we’re especially excited about.




Meet the four

The low earner


Meet Neche. She works hard at a filling station and earns NGN 25,000 a month. Without a solid plan, her money tends to disappear way too quickly. With MyPurse, Neche sets up a daily budgeting schedule that automatically allocates just the right amount for each day—helping her stretch her income and keep spending in check.

The saver


Meet Kenneth, a video editor with big dreams of upgrading his gear. Saving for a new MacBook can feel overwhelming—until he discovered MyPurse. By setting a personalized budgeting plan, Kenneth is now able to chip away at his goal steadily, turning his dream into an achievable journey. Big goals, made simple.

The parent


Meet Mrs. Okpala. Every month, she sends her son NGN 40,000 for school expenses, but the funds always seem to vanish too quickly. With MyPurse, she can schedule smaller, regular transfers throughout the month, teaching him smart spending habits and ensuring he always has just what he needs. Smart money habits start here.

The student


Meet Victor, a university student who knows the struggle of living on a tight budget. Often, he found that his money wouldn’t last until the next payday. With MyPurse, Victor can effortlessly set up a daily spending plan that ensures his essentials are covered—leaving him free to focus on his studies.

Smarter spending, better money habits.
